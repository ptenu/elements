Your membership is provisional for the first four weeks. If your paid via direct debit and we are not able to successfully take a payment during this time, or it comes to light that you are a landlord, then we may reject your membership. If this happens we'll let you know.

After the four weeks, we'll send you a membership card and welcome pack.

We don't use passwords, so to log in you'll need to use a one-time code.

When you enter your email address on the log-in screen, we'll email you three words. These words are selected randomly. Enter the three words in order on the next screen and you'll be logged in.

You can only use these words once and they expire after 15 minutes., next time you log in, new ones will be sent to you.
